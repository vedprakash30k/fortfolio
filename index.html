SONU ENTERPRISES

The SONU ENTERNPRISES CSC VLE is a collective organization of Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs) operating under the Common Services Centre (CSC) framework. It serves as a platform for VLEs to collaborate, share best practices, and address challenges while enhancing their ability to deliver essential digital and financial services to rural and underserved communities.

Our Services

Aadhaar Card

Aadhaar is a unique 12-digit identification number issued by the Indian government to residents based on biometric and demographic data. It serves as a proof of identity and address and is used for various services, including welfare schemes, financial transactions, and government services. Aadhaar helps streamline access to services and ensures transparency in governance.

  • Government Of India

Ration Card

A Ration Card (RC) is a government-issued document in India that provides eligible households access to subsidized food and essentials through the Public Distribution System (PDS). It also serves as a proof of identity and residence.

  • Government Of Bihar

Insurance Policy

An insurance policy is a formal agreement between an individual and an insurance company that offers financial protection against specified risks, such as health issues, accidents, or property damage. In exchange for regular premium payments, the insurer provides compensation or coverage based on the terms of the policy. Insurance policies can be in various forms, including life, health, auto, and home insurance, offering security and helping manage unexpected financial burdens.

  • Government Of India
